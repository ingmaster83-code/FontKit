🔤 What is WooaFont?

WooaFont is a curation service that hand-picks free Korean and English fonts available for commercial use without copyright concerns, organized neatly by category.

From popular Korean fonts like Nanum Gothic, Pretendard, and Noto Sans KR, to English fonts like Inter, Poppins, and Montserrat — we connect you to safe downloads via official distribution channels.

✅ Service Features

  • Only free fonts approved for commercial use are listed
  • Links to official sources (Google Fonts, Naver, GitHub, etc.)
  • 5 categories: Korean Gothic, Korean Serif, English Sans-Serif, English Serif, Display
  • Regularly updated with newly discovered popular fonts
  • Accessible on all devices — PC, tablet, and smartphone
