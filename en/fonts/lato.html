About this Font

Lato by Łukasz Dziedzic is the second-most downloaded font on Google Fonts worldwide. Named after the Polish word for 'summer,' it supports 18 styles from Thin to Black, balancing warmth with professional credibility.

Recommended Use Cases

  • Corporate websites & landing pages
  • Email newsletters
  • UI & dashboards
  • Presentations

Web Font CSS

@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Lato:wght@300;400;700;900&display=swap');
