About this Font

Jeju Gothic is a free commercial Korean font by Jeju Special Self-Governing Province. Inspired by Jeju's nature and culture, it has a warm and distinctive character. Available on Google Fonts, it suits regional branding and expressive content.

Recommended Use Cases

  • Regional & emotional branding
  • Travel & tourism content
  • Culture & art events
  • Character-rich headlines
  • Social media content

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Jeju Gothic commercially?

Yes, Jeju Province distributes it for free commercial use — available for personal and commercial purposes.

Is there only one weight?

Yes, only Regular is available. It is mainly used for headlines and accent text.

No. This page only provides font information and a download link. No files are transmitted to any server.
