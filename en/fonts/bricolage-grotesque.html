About this Font

Bricolage Grotesque by Mathieu Triay is an optical-size variable font, optimizing letterforms from 12pt to 96pt. Its distinctive grotesque character made it one of the leading design trends of 2023 — ideal for brands with a strong personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Bricolage Grotesque commercially?

Yes, under OFL — free for personal and commercial use.

What is Optical Size?

Optical-size variable fonts automatically adjust stroke weight and spacing based on font size, providing optimal readability at any size.
