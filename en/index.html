WooaFont — Free Commercial Font Curation

WooaFont is a curation service that hand-picks free Korean and English fonts available for commercial use without copyright concerns, organized neatly by category. From popular Korean fonts like Nanum Gothic, Pretendard, and Noto Sans KR, to globally beloved English fonts like Inter, Poppins, and Montserrat — find them all in one place.

Korean Gothic fonts can be used across diverse media including websites, apps, and presentations. Pretendard in particular has become the most widely used Korean font in recent years. Korean Serif fonts — Nanum Myeongjo, Noto Serif KR, KoPub Batang — offer publishing-quality typefaces available free of charge.

Among English sans-serif fonts, Inter has become the standard for UI design, and Roboto is the most downloaded font in the world. Poppins and Montserrat are especially well-suited for branding and marketing materials. For English serif fonts, Playfair Display, Lora, and Merriweather deliver elegant and comfortable reading experiences.

All fonts can be safely downloaded via official distribution channels — Google Fonts, Naver Hangeul Hangeul Areumdapge, GitHub, and more. Always verify each font's license (OFL, Apache 2.0, etc.) before commercial use.
